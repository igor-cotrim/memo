# Impeccable Design Context

## Design Context

### Users
Students, language learners, and general knowledge seekers. They use this app during focused study sessions — likely late-night, on a desk or couch, needing concentration and a sense of progress. The interface should feel like a calm, purposeful tool, not a social app.

### Brand Personality
**Focused, calm, rewarding.** The app earns trust by being quiet and reliable. It rewards consistency with visible progress, not flashy celebration. Every interaction should feel intentional and smooth.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Theme**: Dark — study sessions happen in low light, and a dark interface reduces eye strain for focused work.
- **Tone**: Refined minimal dark. Not cold/technical, not glassmorphism-heavy. Purposeful use of color only where it adds meaning.
- **Anti-patterns to avoid**: Cyan-on-dark AI aesthetics, glowing accents everywhere, bouncy animations, badge/gamification overload.
- **Fonts**: Sora (display) + DM Sans (body) — acceptable for this project since they're already established, but new font introductions should avoid the reflex list.
- **Accent**: Blue (#3aa6df) — use sparingly; it should signal interactivity and progress.

### Design Principles
1. **Calm focus over stimulation** — reduce visual noise; let the content breathe.
2. **Progress is the reward** — stats, streaks, and progress bars should feel satisfying without being garish.
3. **Every interaction has a response** — hover, focus, active, loading states must be present everywhere.
4. **Spacing creates hierarchy** — use varied whitespace to signal importance, not color alone.
5. **Motion serves purpose** — transitions should feel smooth and natural; never decorative or distracting.
